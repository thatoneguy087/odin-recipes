Simple Diet Friendly Yogurt Recipe

Super simple recipe for diet friendly yogurt. You only need 2 ingredients that you might already have. This recipe is very customizable so go nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 tub of fat free greek yogurt
  • 1 drink mix packet
  • Mix-ins (optional)

Steps

  1. Open your fresh tub of yogurt
  2. Add about half of your drink mix packet to the tub
  3. Mix well. Be sure to get down in there.
  4. Taste the yogurt. Add more drink mix to taste.
  5. Add your mix-ins (if any)
  6. Serve and enjoy.

Notes

  • Using a whole drink mix packet usually does the trick.
  • Fruity flavor like strawbery works best, but feel free to use whatever sounds good.
  • You can just store your prepared tub in the fridge.
  • This genuinely tastes good, I promise.
