Potato Wedges

Simple but Tasty Potato Wedge Recipe

Super simple but tasty potato wedge recipe. The base for this recipe is Super simple. Great side item or snack. I recommend preparing a main dish while this is in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 Russet Potato
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Additional Seasonings (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Wash potato.
  3. Cut potato into eight wedges.
  4. Toss potatos in a medium bowl with about a tablespoon of oil and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Lay potatoes on baking tray, evenly spaced out.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  7. Serve and dig in.

Notes

  • One potato is generally a good portion size, but use two or more if you're really feeling spicy.
  • I recommend using an airfryer with the default french fry setting if you have it.
  • Dash salt-free seasoning is really good on these.
